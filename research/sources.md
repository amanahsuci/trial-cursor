## Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on three filters:

1. **Active practitioners** — they create their own content, not just advise others
2. **Proven results** — their YouTube or content strategy has produced measurable business outcomes
3. **B2B relevance** — their focus is specifically on B2B SaaS, not general creator or consumer content
