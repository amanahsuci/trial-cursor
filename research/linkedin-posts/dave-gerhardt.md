## Post 2 — April 2026

Exit Five CMO Council — building the best community for B2B VPs and CMOs. Now 60+ members, all VPs and CMOs leading departments at companies doing $50M+ in revenue per year.

The insight behind it: CMOs are lonely at the top. No one on the team understands the strategic world they operate in, and other executives aren't marketers. Peer communities exist to fill that gap — a space to meet CMO peers, talk shop, and get outside perspective.

**Topic:** Why senior B2B marketing leaders need peer communities — the isolation problem at the VP/CMO level and how Exit Five is solving it.
