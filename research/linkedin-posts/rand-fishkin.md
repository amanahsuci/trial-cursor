## Post 2 — April 2026

Step 1: Export all your Alertmouse alerts (mentions of your brand/product)
Step 2: Upload to Claude with a prompt asking it to compare your site's content vs. the questions, uncertainties, and skepticism of reviewers, commenters, press, et al.
Step 3: Address those concerns in your content, messaging, and marketing
Step 4: PROFIT

That's the use-case Scott Heimendinger wrote in to us about, and hot damn if it isn't one of the best ideas we've heard for making Alertmouse data even more actionable. 🔔🐭💯💯💯
