## Post 3 — April 2026

The biggest lie in content marketing is the "AI content machine" that automatically turns one big piece of content into ten small ones.

At Ahrefs, they do the opposite — turn SMALL into BIG:
1. Start with a LinkedIn post. Test the idea. See if anyone cares.
2. If it resonates, turn it into a full article. Use comments as your brief.
3. Take your five best articles and rework them into a conference talk.
4. Discuss that presentation on a podcast.
5. Take a few presentations and you have a video course.

With every step you add more: more signal, more context, more depth.

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/timsoulo_death-by-clawd-have-you-checked-if-your-share-7450751083091505152-D4bG

**Topic:** Content strategy framework — build from small to big, not big to small. Test ideas on LinkedIn first before investing in long-form.
